Welcome to Informalyze

Thank you for installing Informalyze.
This project was made at Deltahacks IV with one purpose in mind: To speed up access to information.
Informalyze has a very simple to use UI.
Simply right click on any higlighted word and click the "Informalyze" option.
A new window will open holding search results from Wikipedia and Wolfram Alpha.
At the bottom of this new window, are tabs that will allow you to switch between these two sources of information.
We are proud to demonstrate this project and hope that in the future, we can build upon it to create and even better product.
